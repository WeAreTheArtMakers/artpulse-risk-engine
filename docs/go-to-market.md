# Customer Acquisition Plan (Web + Outbound + Demo Funnel)

## ICP (first 50 targets)

- Crypto exchanges
- On/off-ramp payment providers
- Wallet/custody providers
- Fintech teams with fraud operations and model governance needs

## Positioning statement

"We help risk teams ship model updates safely in production with KPI-gated rollout and fast rollback containment."

## Funnel design

1. Landing page
- Single message: safe model change in production.
- One CTA: 20-minute Technical Risk Scan.

2. Discovery call (20 min)
- Deliver risk heatmap and gate-fail rollback scenario.

3. 1-week pilot
- Integrate event feed.
- Run canary and baseline KPI comparison.

4. Paid pilot / annual plan
- Add team runbooks, reporting cadence, and SLO governance.

## Outbound sequence

Email 1:
- Subject: "Reduce fraud model release risk in production"
- Message: FPR cost + containment risk + 20-minute scan CTA.

Email 2 (3 days later):
- Subject: "Live gate-fail rollback drill for your risk stack"
- Message: show 3-layer rollback and expected output.

LinkedIn DM:
- Target personas: Head of Risk, Fraud Ops Lead, ML Platform Lead.
- One CTA only: book the 20-minute scan.

## Proof assets for conversion

- Public status page with uptime checks
- Live docs and token demo flow
- Rollback runbooks and incident drill template
- KPI scorecard template
